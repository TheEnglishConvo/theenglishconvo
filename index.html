About

History

The English Convo (TEC) was founded in August of 2021, with the principles of Leadership, Integrity, Diversity, Respect, Kindness, and Teamwork. Since then, our team has continued their commitment and helped English learners around the world reach their potential and break through their fluency.

Who we are

A group of inspired youth passionate about sharing knowledge and promoting mutual understanding between nations throughout the world.
